The Problem

In Bexar County, along with some parts of Medina and Atascosa counties, the San Antonio Water System serves about 1.6 million people every day. When most of us see SAWS, we automatically think, fresh drinkable water. What most of us do not realize is that the San Antonio Water System also take care of sewage. Deadly hazardous waste water waste that if they overflow, cause environmental and health dangers.
